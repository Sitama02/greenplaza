Our Community

GPSC welcomes members of all ages, backgrounds, and fitness levels.

A Diverse Community

At GPSC, we pride ourselves on creating an inclusive environment where everyone feels welcome. Our membership spans all age groups, from children taking their first steps in sports to seniors maintaining active lifestyles.

With a near-equal gender distribution and members from various backgrounds, GPSC truly represents the diverse community of Alexandria.

  • Age Range: 4-75 years
  • Gender Distribution: 55% male, 45% female
  • Family Memberships: 40% of our members
  • Competitive Athletes: 25% of our community
