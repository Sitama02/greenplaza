Specialized Training Programs

Discover our wide range of programs designed for all ages and skill levels.

The Barn Hybrid Gym

Our state-of-the-art fitness facility offers comprehensive training programs for all fitness levels, from beginners to elite athletes. The Barn combines traditional strength training with functional fitness and specialized equipment to deliver results-driven workouts.

Program Offerings:

  • Personal Training: One-on-one sessions with certified trainers
  • Group Classes: High-intensity interval training, strength, and conditioning
  • Specialized Programs: Sport-specific training for athletes
  • Nutrition Guidance: Personalized meal planning and nutritional advice

Equipment Showcase:

  • Olympic weightlifting platforms
  • Functional training rigs
  • Cardio machines and free weights
  • Recovery and mobility tools

Real Madrid Football Academy

Our official Real Madrid Foundation football school teaches the methodology and values of one of the world's most successful clubs. Players develop technical skills, tactical awareness, and physical conditioning under the guidance of UEFA-certified coaches.

Program Levels:

  • Foundation (Ages 4-6): Introduction to football fundamentals through fun games
  • Development (Ages 7-12): Technical skill building and basic tactical concepts
  • Performance (Ages 13-16): Advanced techniques and competitive play
  • Elite (Ages 17+): High-performance training for competitive players

Program Features:

  • Real Madrid curriculum and methodology
  • Video analysis and performance tracking
  • Competitive leagues and tournaments
  • Potential for international exchanges

DOJO Egypt Martial Arts

Our premier martial arts academy offers training in multiple disciplines under the guidance of internationally certified instructors. DOJO Egypt focuses on technical proficiency, mental discipline, and physical conditioning for practitioners of all ages.

Disciplines Offered:

  • Karate: Traditional Japanese martial art focusing on strikes
  • Judo: Olympic grappling and throwing art
  • Taekwondo: Korean martial art emphasizing kicks
  • MMA: Mixed martial arts combining various disciplines

Program Features:

  • Belt progression system
  • Competition preparation
  • Self-defense programs
  • Children's character development

Swimming Academy

Our comprehensive swimming program caters to all ages and abilities, from beginners learning water safety to competitive swimmers refining their technique. All classes are conducted in our temperature-controlled Olympic-sized pool under the supervision of certified instructors.

Program Levels:

  • Water Babies (Ages 6 months-3 years): Parent-child water introduction
  • Learn to Swim (Ages 3+): Fundamental swimming skills
  • Stroke Development: Technique refinement in all four strokes
  • Competitive Training: Advanced training for competitive swimmers

Program Features:

  • Small class sizes for personalized attention
  • Video analysis for technique correction
  • Competition opportunities
  • Adult fitness swimming
View Schedule

Elevate Padel Academy

Our IPF-accredited padel tennis academy offers professional coaching, tournaments, and social play for all skill levels. Elevate Padel combines technical instruction with tactical awareness to help players improve their game in this fast-growing sport.

Program Offerings:

  • Introduction to Padel: Basics for beginners
  • Intermediate Development: Technique refinement and strategy
  • Advanced Coaching: High-level tactical and technical training
  • Social Leagues: Organized play for all levels

Program Features:

  • IPF-certified coaches
  • Video analysis
  • Regular tournaments
  • Court booking system
