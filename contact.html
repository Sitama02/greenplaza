Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about GPSC.

What are your membership options?

We offer daily passes, monthly memberships, and annual memberships with various benefits. Visit our Membership page for detailed information on pricing and included features.

Do you offer programs for children?

Yes, we have programs for children of all ages, starting from as young as 4 years old. Our youth programs include swimming, football, basketball, gymnastics, and more.

How can I book a facility?

Facility booking can be done through our reception desk, by phone, or through our online booking system. Members receive priority booking privileges.

Are there certified coaches for all sports?

Yes, all our programs are led by certified coaches with extensive experience in their respective sports. Many of our coaches have international certifications and competitive backgrounds.

A multi-sport experience that builds a stronger, inclusive community.
