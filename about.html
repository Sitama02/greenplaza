About Us

Our Mission

At Green Plaza Sports Complex, we believe in the power of sports to transform lives and build communities. Our mission is to provide a world-class, inclusive environment where athletes of all ages and abilities can train, compete, and grow together.

Our Vision

"Do it together. Your own way."

This philosophy guides everything we do at GPSC. We recognize that every athlete has unique goals and needs, and we're committed to supporting each individual journey while fostering a sense of community and shared purpose.

Located in the heart of Alexandria, our 6,000m¬≤ facility serves over 1.6 million members annually, offering state-of-the-art training spaces, expert coaching, and a vibrant community atmosphere that inspires excellence and enjoyment in equal measure.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do at GPSC.

Community

Building connections and fostering a sense of belonging for all members.

Excellence

Striving for the highest standards in facilities, coaching, and service.

Inclusivity

Creating a welcoming environment for athletes of all ages, abilities, and backgrounds.

Innovation

Continuously improving our programs, facilities, and approaches to sports training.

A multi-sport experience that builds a stronger, inclusive community.
